Technical Specifications

  • Processor: ARM Cortex-M4 32-bit processor running at 80MHz.
  • Wireless Capabilities: Supports 2.46GHz and 5GHz Wi-Fi signal detection.
  • Power Efficiency: Optimized to operate under 0.75 lb with low power consumption.
  • Operating Conditions: Designed to function in high-altitude environments with varying temperatures.

Background

This project was made for the Georgia Tech NASA Mini Payload competition. So far, we were one of four teams to progress to the CDR (Construction and Design Round) and receive a $100 stipend.

Criteria

  • Must not exceed 0.75 lb
  • Must be fully self-contained and less than 3.7 inches in height
  • Signal Processing
  • Soldering and Prototyping

Key Features

  • ✔ Changing brightness based on signal strength
  • ✔ Wireless
  • ✔ Able to pick up 2.46GHz and 5GHz signals
